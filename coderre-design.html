Senior Design Leadership, Scoped to What You Actually Need.

Coderre Design is my focused practice for product teams, founders, and agencies who need senior-level design without the overhead. I scope engagements tightly, deliver clear artifacts your team can maintain, and get out of the way when the work is done.

What I Offer.

Three focused services, each scoped to deliver clear outcomes without open-ended timelines.

Accessibility Audits.

A structured evaluation of your site or application against WCAG 2.2 and 3.0 guidelines, delivered as a detailed report with prioritized recommendations your team can act on immediately. I test with assistive technology, review the markup by hand, and flag the issues that automated tools miss. You get a clear picture of where you stand and a practical path to compliance — not a generic checklist.

Design System Setup or Review.

Whether you are starting from scratch or inheriting a system that has drifted, I will help you establish a solid foundation. That means token architecture, a component inventory, usage documentation, and governance guidelines that keep things consistent as your team scales. If you already have a system, I audit what exists, identify the gaps, and deliver a roadmap to close them.

Brand + Web Design.

The full identity-to-launch package — logo, type system, color, layout, and a production-ready site built on clean, semantic HTML and CSS. I have been doing this work for over a decade across startups, nonprofits, and enterprise clients. You get a cohesive brand and a fast, accessible website that you own outright and your team can maintain without a designer on retainer.

How It Works.

Every engagement follows four steps. Clear scope, visible progress, and a clean handoff.

1. We Talk

You tell me what you are dealing with. I ask questions, listen, and figure out whether I am the right fit. This costs you nothing.

2. Scope It

I put together a clear proposal with deliverables, timeline, and cost so you know exactly what you are getting before anything starts.

3. Build

I do the work, solve the core problems, and check in at regular intervals so progress stays visible and decisions stay clear.

4. Handoff

You get clean, usable artifacts your team can work with immediately, without a drawn-out process or endless revision loops.
