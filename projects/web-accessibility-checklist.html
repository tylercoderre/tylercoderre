Web Accessibility Checklist.

A practical, Figma-based checklist to help teams ship more accessible experiences without slowing down delivery.

This checklist is built for real project timelines. It focuses on the decisions designers and engineers make every day, from color contrast and focus states to headings, forms, and motion. The goal is to remove guesswork and give teams a shared baseline.

What It Covers.

  • Core WCAG considerations translated into design and UI checks.
  • Keyboard, focus, and interaction states that impact real users.
  • Content and hierarchy rules that improve screen reader navigation.

Get the 2.0 Checklist.

Use it as a pre-launch sweep, a design review checklist, or a shared reference in critiques. It is meant to be adapted to your team and process.
