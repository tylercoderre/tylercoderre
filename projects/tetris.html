Instructions.

Stack pieces to clear lines while keeping the pile below the top.

  • Move: Left/Right arrow keys or the on-screen arrows.
  • Rotate clockwise: Up arrow, D key, or Rotate CW button.
  • Rotate counter-clockwise: A key or Rotate CCW button.
  • Drop: Down arrow or Spacebar.
  • Start / Pause: Start/Pause button or P key.
  • Reset: R key or Reset button.
