Framing the Work for Green Power Energy.

At A Glance: Served as the solo design lead for a full brand refresh and responsive website redesign for Green Power Energy. The work unified brand identity, simplified service messaging, and improved the path to lead conversion. I partnered with an independent developer to implement the final experience.

This project required senior-level decision-making across positioning, information architecture, and conversion UX. I worked directly with stakeholders to align on goals, reduce ambiguity, and turn complex clean energy offerings into a clear, customer-first story that builds trust quickly.

I treated the website like a product. I defined page patterns, content hierarchy, and interaction behavior so the experience stayed consistent across residential and commercial entry points, while keeping key calls to action visible and low friction.

What made this work successful: clarity-first messaging, a scalable visual system, and a layout strategy designed to support marketing growth without needing a redesign every time content changes.

Role: Brand + UI/UX Designer (Solo Design Lead, Strategy Through Execution).

Partners: Independent Developer (Implementation Partner).

Scope: Brand Refresh, Logo System, Responsive Website UX + UI, And Content Structure.

Tools: Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop, And Production-Ready Web Handoff Specs.

Constraints.
  • Trust + Credibility: communicate expertise in energy solutions without overwhelming customers.
  • Multi-Audience Needs: serve residential and commercial prospects with clear paths.
  • Conversion Focus: guide visitors toward contact and estimate requests quickly.
What I Owned.
  • Brand identity system, logo design, and usage guidance.
  • Website information architecture and layout strategy.
  • UX flows for service exploration and lead capture.
  • Visual system for typography, color, and UI components.
Outcomes.
  • A modernized brand that feels clean, confident, and approachable.
  • A responsive website that clarifies offerings and improves lead flow.
  • A scalable visual system ready for ongoing marketing use.

Logo and Identity System.

The identity refresh focused on clarity, balance, and modern energy cues. The full logo and mark were designed to scale across signage, vehicles, and digital touchpoints without losing legibility.

Brand Guide Foundations.

The brand guide set the tone for color, typography, and logo usage. It also defined how the brand scales across marketing, digital, and field applications.

Process and Strategic Decisions.

I started with discovery to align expectations and reduce rework. That included stakeholder interviews, a content and SEO audit of the existing site, and competitor benchmarking to understand category norms and differentiation opportunities.

From there, I rebuilt the information architecture around the questions prospects actually ask. What services do you offer. Who is this for. Why should I trust you. What is the next step. The design intentionally brings those answers forward with clear hierarchy and repeated conversion moments.

To keep the system scalable, I designed modular page sections and a repeatable component approach. This made it easier to add new services, landing pages, and content over time while preserving brand consistency and accessibility best practices.

Homepage Storytelling.

The homepage balances credibility with conversion. It introduces the brand promise, highlights services, and quickly funnels users toward contact and estimates.

Residential Conversion Journey.

Residential pages were structured to answer common questions fast, then guide people into quotes and consultations with minimal friction.

About Page With Trust Signals.

The About experience builds confidence through team credibility, company history, and proof points that reinforce long-term reliability.

Content and Publishing System.

The blog system supports ongoing education and SEO while keeping the UI consistent with the core brand experience.

Kitchen Sink System View.

A full-system view documents the reusable modules and component patterns used across the site for consistency and speed.

Results and Impact.

The redesign delivered a clearer, more credible brand presence and a conversion-oriented website experience. Services are easier to understand at a glance, and key calls to action are consistently positioned so visitors always know what to do next.

Trust Signals

Improved credibility through clearer messaging, a modern visual system, and structured proof points.

Conversion Readiness

Clear CTAs and low-friction pathways for estimates and contact requests across key pages.

Scalability

Modular sections and reusable patterns that support future marketing and content growth.

Delivery

Production-ready handoff aligned with implementation constraints for a smooth build.

If you want metrics, I can walk through the before and after performance indicators that were tracked post-launch, including engagement trends and lead flow signals.
