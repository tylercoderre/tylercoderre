Accessibility.

I am dedicated to making this site fully accessible, starting with AA and hitting AAA when possible so this site's content accessible and user friendly to everyone. This site is a work in progress and may not meet every WCAG item at a given moment. I am constantly learning and striving to keep up with both design and development accessibility considerations. If you are having difficulty viewing or navigating the content on this website, or notice any content, feature, or functionality that you believe is not fully accessible to people with disabilities, please reach out.

I take your feedback seriously and will consider it as I evaluate ways to accommodate all customers and visitors. Additionally, while I do not control certain vendors, I strongly encourage vendors of third-party digital content to provide content that is accessible and user friendly.
